Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe will help you create a delicious homemade cheese and tomato pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Grated Cheddar
  • 4 Large Tomatos
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Create Batter
  2. Spread into Pizza Shape
  3. Mush Tomatos
  4. Spread Tomatoes on Base
  5. Sprinkle Grated Cheese
  6. Bake In Over
  7. Eat Yummy Pizza
