Midwest Salisbury Steak Recipe

Description

This recipe will help you create a delicious homemade Midwest Salisbury Steak

Ingredients

  • Ground Sirloin
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • milk

Steps

  1. Get Ingredients
  2. Make Patties
  3. Heat Skillet
  4. Make Gravy
  5. Add Beef Stock and water
  6. Place Patties into Gravy
  7. Cook
