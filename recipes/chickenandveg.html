Sheet Pan Parmesan Chicken and Veggies Recipe

Description

This recipe will help you create a delicious homemade Chicken and Veg Dish

Ingredients

  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried Parsley
  • Paprika
  • Red Potato

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Create Spice Blend
  3. Create Veg Dish with Olive Oil
  4. Flour into bowl and mix
  5. Slice Chicken into strips
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan
  7. Cook
